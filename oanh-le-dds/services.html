Services

Nine ways we keep your smile shining.

Dentistry is founded on prevention — but life happens. Here's the full range of care available in one office, from routine visits to urgent situations.

Cleaning & Prevention

Dentistry is founded on the principle of prevention. All patients are recommended biannual cleanings and examinations to catch small issues before they become big ones.

Learn more →

Mouthguards

Excessive jaw clenching and bruxism can accelerate tooth and jaw deterioration. A custom-fitted mouthguard protects your teeth while you sleep or play sports.

Learn more →

Cosmetic Dentistry

Cosmetic dentistry can include teeth whitening and much more. Dr. Le offers patients the chance to achieve the gorgeous smile they've always wished for.

Learn more →

Pediatric Dentistry

Children need the right start with oral health care. Dr. Le offers young patients in San Mateo, CA the best beginnings — gentle, patient, and reassuring.

Learn more →

Dental Implants

A dental implant mimics the form and function of a natural tooth — a root and a crown. The crown is the visible, working part, and is built to last.

Learn more →

Dentures

Dentures can restore a patient's smile and give them confidence again. Dr. Le offers patients custom dentures that look completely natural.

Learn more →

Sleep Apnea Treatment

Sleep apnea treatment involves the use of customized oral appliances. These devices can help a patient sleep safely and comfortably through the night.

Learn more →

Tooth Extraction

We make every effort to preserve your natural teeth. Extraction becomes necessary when decay has made a tooth unsalvageable or gum disease has advanced.

Learn more →

Urgent Care

Knocked-out teeth or unusual bleeding from the mouth? Dr. Le is there for patients with urgent dental situations — call and we'll do our best to see you same-day.

Learn more →
