Meet Your Dentist

Dr. Oanh Le, D.D.S.

Dr. Le earned her D.D.S. from Northwestern Dental School, then completed a two-year residency in Advanced Education in General Dentistry at UCSF before opening her San Mateo practice. She's been a Health Sciences Clinical Professor at the UCSF School of Dentistry since 1995 — which means the techniques she uses on you are the same ones she's teaching the next generation of dentists.
