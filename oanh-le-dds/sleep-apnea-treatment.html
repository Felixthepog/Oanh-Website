Sleep Apnea Treatment

Sleep apnea treatment at Dr. Le's office in San Mateo, CA involves the use of customized oral appliances — devices that help a patient sleep safely and comfortably through the night.

What is an oral appliance?

A custom-fitted device worn during sleep to keep the airway open and prevent breathing interruptions. Some focus on airflow, others on jaw position — Dr. Le determines the best fit for you.

Who should get an oral appliance?

Otherwise healthy patients without jaw malformation tend to benefit most, especially those under 65. More severe cases may need to pair an appliance with CPAP therapy.

What's the advantage of a custom appliance over a generic one?

Molded to your mouth, a custom appliance rarely shifts and works at peak effectiveness all night — something generic devices can't match.
