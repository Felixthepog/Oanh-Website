Dentures

Dentures can restore a patient's smile and give them confidence again. Dr. Le offers patients in San Mateo, CA custom dentures that look completely natural.

What are partial dentures?

Partial dentures are made for people missing some teeth who still have healthy teeth remaining. The healthy teeth support the partial denture, which fills the gaps — preventing the shifting, root damage, and alignment issues that can occur when gaps are left unfilled. The base is flexible and suctions to the gums, with acrylic or porcelain teeth that blend naturally with your own.

What are full dentures?

Full dentures are a complete teeth replacement for patients who have lost all their teeth or undergone full-mouth extraction. The base suctions to the roof of the mouth or lower gumline, and is custom-built from a model of your mouth for the closest possible match to your natural smile.

How long do dentures last?

Most people have their dentures for at least a decade, sometimes longer. They may need relining every few years for the best fit, and more rarely a full rebase if your mouth shifts significantly over time.
