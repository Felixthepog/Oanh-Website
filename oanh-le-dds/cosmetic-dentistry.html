Cosmetic Dentistry

Cosmetic dentistry can include teeth whitening and much more. Dr. Le offers patients in San Mateo, CA the chance to achieve the gorgeous smile they've always wished for.

What is cosmetic dentistry?

There are many cosmetic dentistry procedures available today — teeth whitening, dental veneers, dental implants, and dental crowns are all popular options. The goal is always to enhance the smile as a whole, and many patients opt for more than one procedure at a time.

Will insurance cover cosmetic dentistry procedures?

This varies by insurance company. In most cases, dental insurance won't cover procedures done purely for cosmetic reasons, though some procedures are both functional and cosmetic — a dental implant, for instance, is often a structural necessity that also restores your smile. It's worth asking your insurer about coverage, and we can also discuss finance plans that split payments over time.

What's included in a smile makeover?

A smile makeover combines several cosmetic procedures, often performed at the same time — though it may be staged if teeth need to be removed, allowing time to heal between steps. Makeovers often include veneers, crowns, implants, and whitening, resulting in a gap-free smile that looks completely natural.
