Pediatric Dentistry

Children need the right start with oral health care. Dr. Le offers young patients in San Mateo, CA the best beginnings — gentle, patient, and reassuring.

How old should a child be at their first dental visit?

According to the American Dental Association, children should see the dentist at the time of first tooth eruption or on their first birthday, whichever comes first. Even before teeth appear, proper gum care matters.

What should parents look for in a dentist?

Look for a dentist with experience treating patients of all ages, who is patient and compassionate with young patients. Many children feel some fear at the dentist's office, but Dr. Le has plenty of ways to put them at ease and make the visit feel like an adventure rather than something to dread. The most important thing parents can do is establish good routines early — brushing twice a day, two minutes at a time, with small non-food rewards to help kids stay on track.
