Pediatric Dentistry

Children need the right start with oral health care. Dr. Le offers young patients in San Mateo, CA the best beginnings — gentle, patient, and reassuring.

How old should a child be at their first dental visit?

The American Dental Association recommends a first visit at first tooth eruption or by their first birthday, whichever comes first.

What should parents look for in a dentist?

A dentist who's patient and experienced with young patients. Dr. Le has plenty of ways to put kids at ease. At home, the best thing parents can do is build good routines early — brushing twice a day for two minutes.
