Dental Implants

Dental implants replace teeth that have been lost due to decay or damage. Dr. Le offers patients in San Mateo, CA dental implants that look and work just like real teeth.

What are dental implants?

A permanent replacement tooth: a metal support surgically implanted in the jaw, topped with a natural-looking prosthetic.

Are dental implants better than dentures?

It depends on the patient. Implants never come out, unlike dentures, which must be removed nightly. They cost more upfront, but many feel the longevity is worth it — Dr. Le can help you decide.

How long do dental implants last?

For most people, a lifetime. The base rarely needs replacement, though the veneer may need replacing roughly every 10 years from normal wear.

What's the recovery process like?

Patients typically eat a soft diet for at least several days as the implant settles into place, then return to their usual routine fairly quickly.
