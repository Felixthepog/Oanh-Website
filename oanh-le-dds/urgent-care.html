Urgent Care

Urgent care dentist Dr. Le is there for patients in San Mateo, CA when they have urgent situations like knocked-out teeth or unusual bleeding from the mouth.

When does Dr. Le offer urgent care?

Any time the office is open. Call ahead so the staff can prepare, and they'll be happy to rearrange the schedule to fit you in as quickly as possible.

When does a dental issue become truly urgent?

Major bleeding, a broken or knocked-out tooth, significant swelling, an object stuck in the teeth, or extreme tooth pain all qualify as urgent. If you're ever unsure, call the office — the staff can help you figure out whether you need to come in.

How should a knocked-out tooth be handled?

Retrieve it right away, touching the roots as little as possible, and rinse it off in water if it's dirty. Preserve it in a glass of full-fat cow's milk (1% and 2% don't have the preservatives needed) until you arrive. Time matters — if you can get to the office within an hour, there's a good chance the tooth can be successfully re-implanted.
