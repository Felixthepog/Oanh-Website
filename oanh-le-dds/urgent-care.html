Urgent Care

Urgent care dentist Dr. Le is there for patients in San Mateo, CA when they have urgent situations like knocked-out teeth or unusual bleeding from the mouth.

When does Dr. Le offer urgent care?

Any time the office is open. Call ahead so the staff can prepare, and they'll be happy to rearrange the schedule to fit you in as quickly as possible.

When does a dental issue become truly urgent?

Major bleeding, a broken or knocked-out tooth, swelling, or extreme pain all qualify. If you're unsure, just call — we'll help you figure it out.

How should a knocked-out tooth be handled?

Retrieve it right away, touching the roots as little as possible. Rinse if dirty, then store it in full-fat milk until you arrive — within an hour gives the best chance of re-implanting it.
