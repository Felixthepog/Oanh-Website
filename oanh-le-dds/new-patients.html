Your first visit

We make sure every new patient feels welcome, safe, and taken care of. Here's what to expect.

  1. Review your history

    We'll go over your dental and medical history together, so nothing gets missed.

  2. Exam & X-rays

    We examine your teeth and take X-rays of your mouth to get the full picture.

  3. Talk through any issues

    We'll explain anything we find in plain terms and walk through your treatment options.

  4. Cleaning, on your schedule

    We typically proceed with a cleaning at a time that's most convenient for you.
