Our Practice

Thirty years of general and cosmetic dentistry in San Mateo.

Dr. Le provides caring, compassionate care across urgent visits, cosmetic dentistry, extractions, pediatric care, implants, mouthguards, dentures, sleep apnea treatment, and routine cleanings.

A practice that started with one patient at a time

Dr. Oanh Le received her Doctor of Dental Surgery (D.D.S.) degree from Northwestern Dental School in 1990. While in school, she developed a passion for advanced education and learning, which led her to complete a two-year residency at the University of California, San Francisco in Advanced Education in General Dentistry. She then established her private practice in San Mateo, CA — the same office she runs today.

Leading-Edge Dentistry

Still learning. Still teaching.

A teaching career, too

Not one to rest on her laurels, in 1995 Dr. Le's interest in education led her to a teaching career at the UCSF School of Dentistry. She holds the position of Health Sciences Clinical Professor there today.

Evidence-based, current

Dr. Le brings decades of experience and current, evidence-based knowledge to her practice — combined with a deep concern for people that shapes how she explains every option.

Function meets aesthetics

Her integrated approach combines the functional and aesthetic sides of dentistry, aiming for a smile that's healthy, predictable, and looks the way you want it to.
